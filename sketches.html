About the Series

Faith and Opportunity is the story of a young man born into a middle-class family who fell into poverty after his father's tragic accident. The sketches depict him battling severe obstacles as he progresses toward his goal. Each piece is a chapter in a journey that is still being written.

Every original sketch is signed by Wilson, numbered, and comes with a unique hashfigure sent privately to the donor. Keep it safe and confidential. The number and hashfigure are your record. You will need them.

Shipping of physical sketches begins Fall 2026, after Wilson resumes at Johns Hopkins.

A Note on Delivery

Digital sketches will be delivered to your email first and will begin as the series develops.

Physical sketches ship from the United States starting Fall 2026, once Wilson resumes at Johns Hopkins. Shipping from Turkey is unreliable, expensive, and subject to unnecessary taxes and delays. US shipping is faster and ensures your sketch arrives properly.

All donors who provided a shipping address at registration will be contacted directly with updates on their sketch.
